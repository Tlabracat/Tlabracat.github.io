Welcome to Tony La Braca Dry Stone Walling

Tony La Braca Dry Stone Walling is a small company based in Chesterfield.

I have been Dry Stone Walling since 2003 and register as a Professional Dry Stone Waller with the Dry Stone Walling Association and have public liability insurance.

The majority of my customers are located in villages and rural areas within and around the Peak District National Park working on agricultural land, small holdings , private dwelling and Cemeteries etc. I also provide bespoke Stone Walling and Landscape gardening services.

I am conscientious and approach all aspects of my work to my best ability and deliver high quality product and finish.
